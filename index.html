What Majors Have the Best Mid-Career Median Salaries?

When choosing a major, it is important to ask yourself what salary you will be looking at in order to get a good return on investment (ROI) on your college degree. While you can find work with any college degree, yet alone find work without one, not everyone has the luxury of not taking out college loans, so finding a job where you can pay them off is crucial to many. In this chart, you will be able to find what types of majors, and what specific majors, give the best salary towards the middle of one's career. Note that this is only median career, so there may be more variance in different fields. More information about starting salaries and the salaries in different percentiles can be found here: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wsj/college-salaries?resource=download.
